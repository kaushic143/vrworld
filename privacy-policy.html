Privacy Policy

While we pride ourselves on being the leading experts in assisting business, we also take your privacy very seriously. We are committed to providing you with unparalleled digital experiences while also ensuring the safety and security of your personal information through our Privacy Policy.

Agreement to the terms and conditions

This privacy statement ("Policy") sets forth how VR Worldwide ("Company," "We," or "VR Worldwide") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information obtained from users ("Users") of the company's various services ("Services"), whether through the company's website, mobile applications, or network telecom operators. Its goal is to safeguard the confidentiality of our clients' personal data, which they have provided to us in order to access our services.
Our privacy policy was created and developed to protect the security and privacy of the sensitive personal data that customers have supplied to us. Customers' sensitive personal data or information has been included in the definition of personal information for the purposes of this policy. The purpose of the policy is to clarify the following:

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company name, when you contact us or use our services. We may also collect information about your business, including financial information, marketing data, and other business-related information.
We collect this information to provide you with our services and ensure that we understand your business needs. The information we collect may be used to

  • ● Provide you with customized solutions to meet your unique business needs
  • ● Develop marketing strategies and initiatives that align with your business goals
  • ● Improve our services and tailor them to meet your needs
  • ● Conduct research and analysis to better understand your business and industry trends
  • ● Communicate with you about our services, updates, and promotions

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to provide you with the services you have requested, such as business planning, financial analysis, marketing strategy, and operational efficiency. We may also use your information to communicate with you about our services, updates, and promotions.

We may also use your information for marketing purposes, such as sending you promotional materials and newsletters. You may opt-out of receiving these communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the communication.

How We Disclose Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to our employees, contractors, and service providers who require the information to perform their duties. We may also disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, such as court orders or government regulations. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information, including firewalls, encryption, and secure servers.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. You may also withdraw your consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of these sites before providing any personal information.

Cookies

We use cookies on our website to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your device and remember your preferences.

We may use both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your device until they expire or are deleted. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

The information generated by the cookie about your use of our website (including your IP address) may be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States or other countries. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf.

By using our website, you acknowledge that your personal information will be transferred to Google and processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.
